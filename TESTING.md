# Get the Aurum88 Protocol testing app

Aurum88 Protocol is distributed **only through the official app stores** — Google Play and
the Apple App Store. There is **no APK to sideload** and no build to download from this repo.
This keeps every install signed, auto-updating, and verifiable.

To join the test, **email [sunny@bull88protocol.com](mailto:sunny@bull88protocol.com)** with:

- the **platform** you're on (Android or iPhone), and
- the **email address** tied to your store account — your **Google account** (Android) or your
  **Apple ID** (iPhone). You'll be added to the tester list for that store.

You'll get a reply with an opt-in link. After that:

---

## Android — Google Play testing

1. Open the **opt-in link** we send you (on the phone, or sign in to the same Google account).
2. Tap **Become a tester** / **Accept the invite**.
3. Tap the **Google Play** link on that page to open the *Aurum88 Protocol* store listing.
4. Tap **Install**. It installs and updates exactly like any Play Store app — new versions
   arrive automatically, no reinstalling.

> If Play still shows an old version right after you opt in, give it a few minutes (and pull
> to refresh in the Play Store) — testing releases can take a little while to propagate.

## iPhone — Apple TestFlight *(coming soon)*

iOS is in development — see [`ios/`](ios/) for the build plan. When the TestFlight beta opens:

1. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store (one-time).
2. Open the **TestFlight invite link** we send you and tap **Accept** → **Install**.
3. Updates arrive through TestFlight automatically.

Email the same address to be added to the TestFlight list when it opens.

## What to expect

- It's a **testing build**: free, as-is, may contain bugs, and may change at any time. See
  [TERMS.md](TERMS.md) and [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
- It's a **bring-your-own-keys** app — it runs on your device with no backend. On first launch the
  **Getting Started** guide explains what each optional key (FRED / Gemini) unlocks.
- **Not financial or investment advice.** Every trading decision is yours alone.
